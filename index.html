A bit about Mr Fin

Mr Fin is a year 7 student at Inner Sydney high school in the inner suburbs of Sydney. Mr Fin is well regarded as being a hard working student by his teachers. However it has been said that Fin's real interest is not at school at all though, and all he really likes is playing video games.

Inner Sydney high school

One of the first things you notice about Inner Sydney is that it's a vertical school. Built in the inner suburbs, land prices are a premium so the school has a very small footprint but is 14 stories high.

Inner Syd has only been in operation for 6 years, when it started it had only year 7 classes. Those kids now make up the first year 12 class and the first graduates will be at the end of this year.
